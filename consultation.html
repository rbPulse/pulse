Consent & Authorization
Telehealth Consent
You are consenting to receive medical consultation services via telehealth. Your clinician will review your intake information, assess eligibility, and recommend a protocol if appropriate.
Prescribing Model
Pulse operates under a licensed MSO/PC model. Prescriptions are issued by licensed clinicians and fulfilled through FDA-registered compounding pharmacies. A prescription is not guaranteed.
Data Handling & HIPAA
Your information is protected under HIPAA and handled with strict confidentiality. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest and is never shared for marketing purposes.
Right to Withdraw
You may withdraw your consent at any time before prescription fulfillment by contacting Pulse support.
Regulatory Status
Some compounds are FDA-approved for specific indications; others are compounded formulations. This service is not diagnostic and does not replace primary care.
