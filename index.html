FAQ

Questions worth asking.
We've got you.

How is this different from other standard peptide programs?
Most peptide programs leave the hard parts to you — sourcing compounds, calculating doses, managing timing, and staying consistent. Pulse handles all of it. Pre-filled pods, a precise auto-injector pen, and a clinically guided protocol mean there's nothing to figure out. You just follow the system.
Do I need a prescription?
Specific requirements vary by compound and location. All protocols are reviewed and authorised through Pulse's MSO/PC clinical model before your first kit ships.
How quickly will I see results?
Sleep and recovery compounds typically produce noticeable effects within 1–2 weeks. Body composition and performance protocols show meaningful results between weeks 4–8, with the most significant changes in months 2–3.
What's actually in the pods? Are they safe?
Every Pulse pod contains a pre-measured, pharmaceutical-grade peptide compound. All compounds are batch-tested for purity and potency before fulfilment.
What if I miss a dose?
Skip it and continue your normal schedule the next day. Do not double-dose.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees.
