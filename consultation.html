Consent and authorization

Review the following before proceeding. Your e-signature confirms you have read and understood each section.

Telehealth consent
You are consenting to receive medical consultation services via telehealth. Your clinician will conduct a live video or audio session to review your intake information, assess eligibility, and recommend a protocol if appropriate. Telehealth services carry the same standard of care obligations as in-person visits.
Prescribing model
Pulse operates through a licensed Medical Services Organization (MSO) and Professional Corporation (PC) model. All prescriptions are issued by licensed physicians. Compounds are compounded by an FDA-registered 503B compounding pharmacy. Pulse does not guarantee that a prescription will be issued following your consultation.
Data handling and HIPAA
Your medical information is protected under the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). Information collected during this intake will be shared only with your treating clinician and the dispensing pharmacy as required to fulfill your care. It will not be sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes.
Right to withdraw
You may withdraw from this process at any time prior to prescription fulfillment without penalty. To cancel after prescription issuance, contact support within 24 hours of confirmation.
Regulatory status of compounds
Some compounds offered by Pulse have FDA approval for specific indications; others are used under physician authorization as compounded medications. These statements have not been evaluated by the FDA for the specific uses described. Individual results vary. Pulse protocols are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.
